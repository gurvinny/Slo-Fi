    Audio Controls
    Speed
    Slows or speeds up playback (0.5× – 1.7×) without changing pitch.
    Pitch
    Shifts pitch up or down by semitones while keeping tempo constant.
    Volume
    Master output level.
    Reverb Mix
    Wet/dry blend of the convolution reverb effect.
    Decay
    How long the reverb tail rings out (0.5s – 8s).
    Room Size
    Virtual room size — affects reverb diffusion and spaciousness.
    EQ
    Three-band equalizer (low shelf, mid peak, high shelf) ±12 dB each.
    Chorus
    Rate controls modulation speed; Depth controls intensity of the shimmer.
    Saturator
    Adds harmonic distortion — from subtle warmth to heavy drive.
    8D Audio
    Binaural panning that rotates the audio around your head. Use headphones.
    Hz Resonance
    Applies a solfeggio frequency tone underneath the track.
    Visual Effects
    Wireframe
    Renders only the orb's mesh edges as glowing lines instead of a solid surface.
    Bass Pulse
    Orb scales up on every kick drum hit.
    Lightning
    Electric arc bolts that spark from the orb surface on beats.
    Crack Veins
    Blazing fracture lines that appear on heavy bass hits.
    Crystallize
    Freezes and ices the orb geometry when playback is paused.
    Glitch
    Digital corruption artifacts — vertex displacement and color noise.
    Reactivity
    How aggressively the orb responds to the audio — low for calm visuals.
    Glow
    Bloom post-processing intensity around the orb.
    Particles
    Number of dust motes orbiting the sphere.
    Stars
    Brightness and twinkle intensity of the background star field.
